Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Thank you for using WonderBlast!. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the WonderBlast! mobile application and related services.

1. Developer Information

WonderBlast! is developed and operated by Dylan Douglas (Independent Developer).

This Privacy Policy applies to the WonderBlast! mobile application and related services.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us using the contact information provided below.

2. Information We Collect

We follow a data minimization principle, meaning we only collect information necessary to operate and improve the application.

2.1 Device and Usage Information

When you use WonderBlast!, certain information may be automatically collected, including:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID such as IDFA or AAID)
  • Coarse IP-based location (country or region level)
  • App usage information (such as gameplay activity and session duration)
  • Interaction with advertisements

This information is used to:

  • Operate and maintain the app
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Measure performance and analytics
  • Display advertisements

2.2 Crash and Diagnostic Information

We may collect crash reports and diagnostic information to help identify and fix technical issues.

This may include:

  • Device model
  • OS version
  • Crash timestamps
  • Error logs

This information is used only for app stability and performance improvements.

2.3 Information You Voluntarily Provide

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it.

For example:

  • Contacting us via email
  • Sending feedback or bug reports

If you contact us, we may keep the information necessary to respond to your request.

3. Advertising

WonderBlast! may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners.

These advertising partners may use device identifiers and usage information to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

You can manage your advertising preferences in your device settings.

For example:

  • Resetting your Advertising ID
  • Limiting Ad Tracking

4. Third-Party Services

WonderBlast! uses third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads.

AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / AAID)
  • Device information
  • Interaction with ads

More information about Google's privacy practices can be found here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Information

5. EEA / UK Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, the app may display a consent form using Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP).

This allows users to:

  • Consent to personalized advertising
  • Choose non-personalized ads
  • Manage privacy preferences

Your choices can be updated when the consent form appears.

6. Children's Privacy

WonderBlast! is intended for a general audience.

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove the information.

7. Data Retention

We retain collected information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Improve services
  • Ensure app stability
  • Comply with legal obligations

Anonymous or aggregated information may be retained longer.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to data you provided
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of information you voluntarily provided
  • Limit or opt out of personalized advertising

Advertising preferences can also be managed through your device settings.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If changes occur, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.

Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
